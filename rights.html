Privacy

Overview

I care about your privacy. I mean, I care about my own privacy as well. I believe in the foundamental concept of privacy as a human right. This is why I will never sell OR share your information to ANY third party. All information is anonymised.

What information is collected, and why

The following information is collected:

  • A unique session code for diffrent visitors, for view counting purposes;
  • What browser/operating system you are using (by viewing your User-Agent header), and;
  • Your screen size.

Why I collect data

The main purpose of the data includes, but not limited to:

  • Seeing how many users visit my site for development purposes (eg. to choose how powerful the server), and;
  • Adjusting to real-world visitor enviornment.

Data retention

The above outlined data is stored for a maximum period of 3 months, afterwhich all related data will be erased.

Collection method

The analytics is collected by GoatCounter, and their open source code can be found on Github. All information is anonymised and we will never traceback to your real user. The data is currently stored on their server, but in the near future this will change and I plan to store data on my own server.

Opt out

At the current stage, I do not have an opt-out option. This is will be implemented as soon as possible. Thank you for your understanding.

Change of this notice

This privacy notice can be changed without further notice, when needed. Last revised Apr 2026.
